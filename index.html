AJENTA LEKH

Ajanta caves are located near Ajanta village, at a distance of 80km from Aurangabad,in the state of Maharashtra and take about 2.5hr to go. These caves were covered with beautiful paintings with the use of a lot of coloursfrom the life of boddha. it's hard to believe but the paintings were done by the monks who had nothing else to do during the rainy season.
